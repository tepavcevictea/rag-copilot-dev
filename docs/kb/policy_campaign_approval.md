### Campaign Approval Policy
**Purpose:** Standardize campaign review and rejection criteria.  
**Scope:** All ad creatives and landing pages.  
**Rules:**  
- Campaign review SLA is **4 business hours** for standard queue.  
- Prohibited content: explicit scams, impersonation, malware, false ROI guarantees.  
- Restricted content requires legal notes and jurisdiction filters.  
- Rejected campaigns include exact rejection code and remediation steps.  
**Exceptions:** Priority enterprise queue SLA is **90 minutes** for pre-vetted clients.  
**Owner:** Trust and Safety  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-30

**Definitions:**
- **Request Window:** Time from invoice issuance during which a dispute/refund can be submitted.
- **Qualified Case:** Case type that satisfies policy and evidence requirements.

**Inputs Required:**
- Account ID and invoice reference
- Campaign IDs and date range
- Evidence artifacts (screenshots, logs, billing export)

**Operational Notes:**
- Support must not promise outcomes before Finance review.
- Any policy override requires documented approver and rationale.

**Audit and Controls:**
- Store all approval/denial decisions in ticket history.
- Monthly spot-check 5% of policy decisions for consistency.
