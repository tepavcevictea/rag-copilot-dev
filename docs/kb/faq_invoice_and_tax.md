### FAQ: Invoices and Tax
Q: When are invoices issued?  
A: Invoices are generated on the **1st of each month** for previous-period spend.

Q: Can invoice details be edited?  
A: Tax ID and legal name updates apply to future invoices, not retroactively.

Q: Is VAT included automatically?  
A: VAT handling depends on jurisdiction and verified tax profile.
