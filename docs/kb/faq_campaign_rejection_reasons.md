### FAQ: Why Was My Campaign Rejected?
Common reasons:
- Unsupported claims (e.g., guaranteed profits).  
- Destination mismatch between ad and landing page.  
- Missing legal disclaimers for regulated regions.  
- Malicious redirects or cloaked final URLs.  
Tips:
- Use transparent ad copy.
- Keep landing page content consistent with creative.
- Include risk disclosure where required.
