### KYC Requirements Policy
**Purpose:** Ensure compliance for high-risk account activity.  
**Scope:** Accounts requesting high spend limits or fiat withdrawals.  
**Rules:**  
- KYC required above **USD 15,000 monthly spend**.  
- Required documents: government ID, proof of address (<90 days), beneficial owner statement.  
- Review target is **2 business days**.  
- Failed verification blocks payout and advanced targeting features.  
**Exceptions:** Existing enterprise contracts may use alternate compliance packet.  
**Owner:** Compliance Team  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05

**Definitions:**
- **Request Window:** Time from invoice issuance during which a dispute/refund can be submitted.
- **Qualified Case:** Case type that satisfies policy and evidence requirements.

**Inputs Required:**
- Account ID and invoice reference
- Campaign IDs and date range
- Evidence artifacts (screenshots, logs, billing export)

**Operational Notes:**
- Support must not promise outcomes before Finance review.
- Any policy override requires documented approver and rationale.

**Audit and Controls:**
- Store all approval/denial decisions in ticket history.
- Monthly spot-check 5% of policy decisions for consistency.
